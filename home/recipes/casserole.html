Noodle Casserole

Ingredients:

  • Box of your favorite noodle shape
  • American Cheese
  • Ketchup

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a pot, boil water.
  3. Sprinkle in a pintch of salt to add taste.
  4. Pour in your favorite noodle shape.
  5. Cook for about 10 minutes or until noodles are soft (taste to test).
  6. Drain the noodles.
  7. Pour the noodles into a pan.
  8. Mix in Ketchup.
  9. Add Pieces of American Cheese to the top (your desired amount).
  10. Cook for about 1/2 hour, and check to see if more cheese/ketchup is needed.
  11. Mix Cheese and Ketchup and serve while hot. Enjoy!
