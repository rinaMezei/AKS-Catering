Delicious Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups of flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons of butter, melted

Directions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar.
  2. In the center, add milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Mix until well blended, with no clumps.
  4. Heat up lightly oiled frying pan.
  5. Scoop out batter into pan (approximately 1/4 cup).
  6. Cook until brown on both sides, flipping when necessary.
  7. Top with your favorite toppings and enjoy!
