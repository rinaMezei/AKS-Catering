Delicious Biscotti

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 1/4 cups of sugar
  • 1 cup of oil
  • 4 cups of flour
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla sugar
  • (optional)1 cup of Chocolate Chips
  • Cinnamon and Sugar for sprinkling

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a mixing bowl, beat eggs, sugar, and oil for about 3 minutes.
  3. Add in flour, baking powder, and vanilla sugar, mixing until well blended.
  4. Mix in chocolate chips if desired.
  5. Put parchment paper onto a baking sheet.
  6. Shape dough into logs (approximately 2 logs).
  7. Sprinkle cinnamon and sugar on the top if desired.
  8. Bake for approximately 20 minutes.
  9. Cut while still hot and enjoy!.
