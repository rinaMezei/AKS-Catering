Ashreinu Kitchen Squad began in Camp Ashreinu, a Kiruv camp in Burks Falls, Ontario. A group of six girls became the camp chefs, under the head cook, Mrs. Webber. Positive feedback of the food began the now world famous Ashreinu Kitchen Squad, located in Toronto, Ontario. Ashreinu Kitchen Squad is a restaurant located in the center of Toronto, and is a popular place for Frum families to come and enjoy a nice, delicious, milchig meal. AKS is also proud to cater your next simcha. We cater Brissim, Bar and Bat Mitzvahs, and many other events.
